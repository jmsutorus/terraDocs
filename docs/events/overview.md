# Events

Events is where you track the things that happen in your life — gatherings, appointments, occasions, and milestones. You can plan them in advance, tag who was there, add photos, and write notes afterward. Over time they build into a searchable, browsable timeline of your social and personal history.

## How it works

Each event has a title, a date, and optionally a time, location, category, and description. From there you can add photos, tag attendees from your People directory, write longer notes in a markdown editor, and link the event to a restaurant visit or sports result. Events show up across four different views depending on how you want to browse them.

## Timing options

- **Timed** — a start time and optional end time
- **All-day** — no specific time, just the date
- **Multi-day** — a start date and an end date for events that span multiple days

## Categories

You define your own event categories — Social, Health, Work, Personal, or anything else that fits your life. Each category gets a color badge that appears throughout the app. You can create, rename, and delete categories at any time.

## The four tabs

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Featured upcoming event, this week's schedule, and coming up next |
| **Collection** | Your full event list with search and category filter |
| **Timeline** | Chronological card view of upcoming and past events |
| **Daily** | An hourly calendar grid for a single day |

## What you can do

- **Create events** with full or minimal detail — a title and date is enough
- **Tag attendees** from your People directory
- **Add photos** and captions
- **Write detailed notes** using the markdown editor (transcripts, recaps, reflections)
- **Link events to restaurant visits** to connect a dining experience to the occasion
- **Filter by category** across all views
- **Publish events** to share them on your public Terra profile

## How events connect to the rest of Terra

People you tag on an event appear in their profile under Shared History. Restaurant visits linked to an event show on both the event and the restaurant's detail page. Events also appear on calendar views across the app and surface in the people profiles of anyone tagged.

*Gather with purpose. Remember the connection.*
