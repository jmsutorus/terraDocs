# Browsing Events

Events has four views, each suited to a different way of looking at your schedule and history.

## Home tab

The Home tab gives you a curated look at what's coming up:

- **Featured event** — the next upcoming event that has a cover photo, shown as a large hero card
- **Weekly events** — the next 7 days as a compact card row, one card per day
- **Coming up** — events beyond the next week as a list
- **Milestone event** — the next event in your schedule that has a photo attached, highlighted separately

Use the Home tab as your daily glance — what's happening this week and what's on the horizon.

## Collection tab

The Collection tab shows all your events — past and future — in a searchable, filterable table.

**Search** — matches against title, description, and location in real time.

**Category filter** — a dropdown to show only events in a specific category. Useful when you want to see only your Work events, or only Social ones.

**Each row shows:**
- Event title
- Date and time
- Category badge (color-coded)
- Location

Tap any row to open the full event detail page.

## Timeline tab

The Timeline tab shows events as cards in chronological order, split into two groups: **Upcoming** and **Past**.

Each card displays:
- The cover photo (if the event has one)
- Title, date, and time
- Location
- Category badge
- Attendee avatar stack (up to 3 shown, with a +N indicator for more)

This view is best for browsing your history visually — scrolling through past events with their photos gives a much richer sense of your archive than a table.

Search and category filter are available here too, so you can narrow the timeline to a specific type of event or keyword.

## Daily tab

The Daily tab shows a single day as an hourly calendar grid — a traditional day planner view.

- All-day events appear in a section at the top
- Timed events are positioned on the grid based on their start and end time
- Overlapping events are displayed in side-by-side columns so nothing is hidden
- Navigate between days with the previous/next buttons

Use the Daily tab when you want to see how a specific day was structured, or when planning a day with multiple events.

## Common questions

**I have a lot of past events. How do I find one from a year ago?**
Use the Collection tab search bar with a keyword from the title, description, or location. Or use the category filter to narrow to the right type, then scroll.

**Which view should I use most?**
Home tab for daily/weekly awareness. Timeline tab for browsing your history visually. Collection tab for finding a specific event quickly. Daily tab when you need an hourly view of a single day.

**Can I see all events for a specific person?**
Go to that person's profile in the People section. Their Shared History shows all events you've tagged them on, with links to each event.
