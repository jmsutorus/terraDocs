# Calendar

The Calendar is a monthly view of your entire life archive. Every piece of data you've logged in Terra — moods, workouts, events, tasks, media, journal entries, restaurant visits, birthdays, vacations, and more — shows up on the day it happened. Click any day to see a detailed summary of everything logged for it.

## How it works

The calendar displays the current month as a grid. Each day cell shows small indicators for what was logged that day. Click a cell and a detail panel opens below the grid, showing the full picture of that day across all your sections.

Navigate backward and forward through months using the arrow buttons. The "Today" button jumps back to the current month at any time.

## What appears on the calendar

Almost everything in Terra is date-stamped and feeds into the calendar:

- Mood entries
- Habit completions
- Workouts (upcoming scheduled and completed)
- Events
- Tasks (by due date — overdue, upcoming, and completed)
- Journal entries
- Media (movies, TV, books, games)
- Restaurant visits
- Drinks logged
- Parks visited
- Vacation itinerary days and bookings
- Goals and milestones (by due or completion date)
- People's birthdays and anniversaries
- Relationship moments
- Federal holidays

## Three sections below the grid

Below the calendar grid, three editorial sections give context for the displayed month:

- **Coming Up** — upcoming events, birthdays, anniversaries, and milestones for the current month as hero cards
- **Past Memories** — a grid of journal entries and media from the displayed month, useful for browsing past months
- **Month Summary** — aggregated stats for the month

## What you can do

- **Browse your history** by month — flip back to any month and see what was happening
- **Click any day** to see a full summary of that day's data
- **Create a new event** from the Add Event button at the top
- **Navigate to a daily detail page** from the day panel for a deep-dive view of a specific date

*Every day, remembered.*
