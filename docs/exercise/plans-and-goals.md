# Plans & Goals

Beyond logging individual sessions, Terra lets you work toward something. This page covers AI-generated workout plans, workout goals, and personal records — all accessible from the **Home** and **Records** tabs.

## AI-generated workout plans

Terra can build a personalized 7-day workout plan based on your goals and preferences. To generate one, go to the **Home** tab and find the plan section.

**How it works:**
1. Answer a short questionnaire about your goals, current fitness level, and preferences
2. Terra generates a structured 7-day plan with a type and intensity for each day, including rest days
3. Review the plan — each day shows the activity type, difficulty, and a brief description
4. Tap **Add to Upcoming** on any day to slot it into your schedule

The plan explains its own balance — why certain days are rest days, how intensity ramps up or tapers — so you understand the reasoning, not just the prescription.

**Re-generating a plan:** If the generated plan doesn't fit, you can answer the questionnaire again to get a new one.

## Workout goals

Workout goals are simple, self-directed targets you set for your movement. Examples:
- "Run a 5K in under 25 minutes"
- "Complete 3 strength sessions per week for a month"
- "Hold a headstand for 30 seconds"

**To add a goal:** Go to the **Home** or **Records** tab and tap **Add Goal**. Write your goal in plain text.

**To mark a goal achieved:** Tap the goal to toggle it to met. Goals stay visible in your list after you achieve them so you can see what you've accomplished.

**To delete a goal:** Use the delete option on any goal entry.

Goals are manual — Terra won't auto-complete them based on your logged workouts. You decide when you've hit the target.

## Personal records (PRs)

The Records tab tracks your athletic milestones. You choose which PR categories to track in settings.

**Available categories:**
- **Running PRs** — fastest times, longest distances
- **Weightlifting PRs** — heaviest lifts per exercise

**To add a PR:** Tap **Add PR** from the Records tab. Enter the exercise or distance, the value (time or weight), and the date you achieved it.

**To edit a PR:** Tap any existing record to update the value or date.

**To enable/disable PR tracking:** Open Exercise Settings and toggle the categories you want.

PRs are logged manually — Terra doesn't auto-detect them from your workout entries. When you hit a new milestone, you add it yourself.

## Common questions

**Does the AI plan connect to my logged workouts?**
Once you add plan days to your upcoming schedule, they appear as scheduled workouts. When you complete one, you log the actual activity as normal — the plan and your logs stay linked so you can see what you planned vs. what you did.

**Can I have more than one active plan?**
One plan displays at a time on the Home tab. You can always generate a new one when you're ready for a change.

**Do goals auto-reset or expire?**
No. Goals stay until you delete them or mark them achieved. There's no expiry.
