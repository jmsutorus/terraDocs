# Tasks

Tasks is a straightforward action-item tracker. Capture things you need to do, set priorities and due dates, organise by category, and track your completion rate over time.

## How it works

Each task has a title, a status, and optionally a priority, category, due date, and description. Tasks appear on your Calendar by due date and in daily views across Terra. Completing a task is a single tap.

Terra also supports **natural language entry** — type "Call dentist Friday high priority" and Terra automatically parses the date and priority from your text.

## Statuses

You can use the default statuses or create your own:

**Default statuses:** Active, In Progress, Blocked, On Hold, Cancelled, Completed

Custom statuses let you build a workflow that fits your process — for example, adding "Waiting On" or "In Review" as states specific to how you work.

## What you can do

- **Create tasks** quickly with the task form, including natural language parsing
- **Set priority** — Low, Medium, or High
- **Assign categories** — custom categories you create and name
- **Set due dates** — tasks appear on your Calendar by due date
- **Toggle completion** with a single tap
- **Create custom statuses** with custom colors for your workflow
- **Bulk-delete completed tasks** to clear the list
- **See productivity trends** in the Velocity chart

## The three tabs

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Quick task entry and your featured active tasks |
| **Database** | Full task list with filtering and sorting |
| **Governance** | Manage your categories and custom statuses |

## How tasks connect to the rest of Terra

Tasks with due dates appear on your Calendar and in daily views. They can also be linked to Goals — connecting a specific action to the larger intention it supports. Overdue tasks surface in the Calendar's day detail panel so nothing slips past silently.
