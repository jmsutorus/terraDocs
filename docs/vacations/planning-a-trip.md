# Planning a Trip

This page covers how to create a vacation, build out the itinerary and bookings, manage your budget, and tag who's coming with you.

## Creating a vacation

Tap the **+** button from the Vacations page.

**Required:**
- **Title** — the name of the trip
- **Destination** — where you're going
- **Start date** and **end date**

**Optional at creation (editable later):**
- **Type** — Beach, Ski, Road Trip, City, Cultural, Camping, etc.
- **Status** — Planning, Booked, In Progress, Completed, or Cancelled
- **Description** — a brief note about the trip
- **Cover photo** — upload a file or paste a URL; used as the hero background throughout
- **Tags** — for filtering and browsing
- **Rating** — 1–10; typically added after the trip is done
- **Budget** — planned total spend, currency, and actual spend once you're back

## The vacation lifecycle

Status reflects where the trip is in its lifecycle:

| Status | When to use it |
|---|---|
| **Planning** | You're still building the itinerary |
| **Booked** | Flights, hotels, and key bookings are confirmed |
| **In Progress** | You're currently on the trip |
| **Completed** | You're back; the trip is done |
| **Cancelled** | The trip didn't happen |

The view on the vacation detail page adapts based on status. Planning and Booked show the itinerary and booking management panels. Completed shifts to a narrative and gallery view.

## Building the itinerary

The itinerary section creates one entry per day of the trip. Each day has:
- **Title** — what you're calling this day ("Travel Day," "Rome," "Day at the Beach")
- **Location** — where you'll be
- **Activities** — a list of what you're doing
- **Notes** — anything extra
- **Photo** — a single image for the day
- **Budget** — planned and actual spend for the day

Days are auto-numbered and dated based on your trip's start date. Add, edit, or delete days as your plans evolve.

## Adding bookings

Bookings track your reservations. For each booking you can record:
- **Type** — Flight, Hotel, Activity, Car Rental, Restaurant, Train, or Other
- **Title** — what the booking is for
- **Date, start time, end time**
- **Confirmation number**
- **Provider** — airline, hotel chain, etc.
- **Location**
- **Cost**
- **Booking URL**
- **Status** — Pending, Confirmed, or Cancelled
- **Notes**

Bookings are grouped by type on the detail page, so all your flights are together, hotels together, and so on.

## Budget tracking

Set a planned total budget when creating the vacation, then track actual spend as you go. You can break spending down to the day level in the itinerary — each day has its own planned and actual fields. The budget panel shows:
- Planned total vs. actual total
- Cost per day (actual spend ÷ days)
- Currency (selectable)

## Tagging people

In the People section of the vacation detail, add anyone who traveled with you. Select from your People directory and assign a relationship type (Family, Friends, Work, Other). They'll appear in the trip's people list and the vacation will show in their shared history on their People profile.

## Notifications

Individual itinerary days and bookings support notification settings — configure reminders for the night before, the day of, or a custom time. Useful for early flights or confirmed reservations that need your attention.
