# Vacations

Vacations is where you plan, track, and archive your trips. It handles the logistics before you leave — itinerary, bookings, budget — and becomes a narrative archive after you're back, with photos, a written account, and a record of who traveled with you.

## How it works

Each vacation starts with a title, destination, dates, and a type. From there it moves through a lifecycle — Planning → Booked → In Progress → Completed — and the view you see adapts to the phase you're in. While planning, you see itinerary and booking management. Once completed, the focus shifts to the narrative, photos, and reflection.

## Vacation types

Beach, Ski, Cruise, Road Trip, City, Camping, Adventure, Cultural, Theme Park, Festival, Business, Staycation, or Other.

## What you can add to a vacation

- **Itinerary** — day-by-day agenda with location, activities, notes, and a daily photo
- **Bookings** — flights, hotels, car rentals, activities, restaurants, trains, and other reservations with confirmation numbers, times, costs, and status
- **Budget** — planned and actual spend at both the vacation level and per day
- **Photos** — multiple photos with captions and dates, displayed as a gallery
- **People** — tag who came with you
- **Written narrative** — a full markdown editor for capturing the story of the trip
- **Rating** — 1–10 for the overall trip
- **Tags** — custom labels for browsing

## What you can do

- **Plan a trip** with a full itinerary and bookings before you leave
- **Track your budget** against what you actually spent
- **Log who traveled with you**
- **Add photos** during or after the trip
- **Write a trip narrative** after you're back
- **Publish your vacation** to share it on your public Terra profile with a shareable link

## How it connects to the rest of Terra

People you tag on a vacation appear in their Terra profile under Shared History. Published vacations include a social sharing panel so you can send the link via WhatsApp, email, or social media.
