# Using Guide

This page covers how to get the most out of Guide — what kinds of questions work well, how to create data through chat, and how to manage your usage quota.

## Starting a conversation

Navigate to the Guide (chat) page and type in the input at the bottom. Press Enter or tap the send button.

Guide maintains conversation history within a session so you can follow up on previous messages — "What about last month?" or "Show me the ones I rated above 8" works in context.

## Asking about your archive

Guide has full read access to your Terra data. Useful question patterns:

**Lookups:**
- "What did I log on March 15th?"
- "Show me my last five journal entries"
- "What's my current streak for the meditation habit?"

**Summaries:**
- "How many workouts did I do in April?"
- "What's my average mood for this month?"
- "Give me a breakdown of my media by type this year"

**Cross-section questions:**
These are where Guide is most valuable — questions that no single section can answer:
- "What was my mood like during my last vacation?"
- "Which of my habits correlates most with Radiant mood days?"
- "What parks have I visited with my family?"

## Creating entries through chat

Guide can create data in Terra directly from a message. Describe what you want:

- "Add a task to call the dentist on Friday with high priority"
- "Log a 45-minute run this morning, moderate difficulty"
- "Create an event called Book Club for next Tuesday at 7pm"
- "Add a journal entry for today titled 'Good day' with a mood of 4"

When Guide creates something, it shows a confirmation card with the created entry's details.

## Attaching images

Tap the **+** button next to the input field to attach an image. Guide can use images as context — useful for things like asking about a photo from a trip, or providing visual context for something you want to describe.

Supported formats: PNG, JPG, WebP, and most standard image types. HEIC/HEIF files are not supported for attachments.

## Managing your quota

The header shows your current model and a quota progress bar:

- **Normal (under 75%)** — no action needed
- **Amber (75–90%)** — getting close; consider switching models
- **Red (90%+)** — nearly at limit; switch models to continue

Use the model dropdown in the header to select a different model. Each model has its own separate quota, so switching resets your available capacity for that session.

## Reading rich cards

When Guide retrieves or creates structured data, it renders it as a card in the conversation:

- **Workout cards** show exercises, duration, difficulty
- **Event cards** show title, date, location, attendees
- **Task cards** show title, due date, priority, status
- **Vacation cards** show destination, dates, rating

These cards are display only — to edit an entry, navigate to its section in the app.

## Common questions

**Can Guide modify existing entries?**
Yes — Guide can update entries you describe. "Change my workout from yesterday to hard difficulty" or "Mark the Buy Groceries task as complete."

**Does Guide remember past conversations?**
Guide has context within your current session. Very long sessions may eventually lose early context as the conversation grows. For best results, start fresh sessions for unrelated topics.

**Can I pre-fill a conversation topic?**
Yes — some sections of Terra link directly to Guide with a pre-filled prompt (like the Habits Consistency Insight). You can also append `?q=your+question` to the Guide URL to start with a specific question.
