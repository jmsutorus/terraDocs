# Guide (AI Chat)

Guide is Terra's AI assistant — a conversational interface that has full context on your Terra archive and can answer questions, surface patterns, and help you create or update entries across all sections.

## How it works

Guide is a chat interface. Type a message and Guide responds. It knows your mood history, habits, workouts, journal entries, media, goals, restaurants, parks, and everything else in your archive — so you can ask questions that cross multiple sections in ways that no individual page can answer.

You can also attach images to your messages for additional context.

## What you can ask or do

**Questions about your data:**
- "Why was I in such a good mood in March?"
- "What restaurants have I visited the most this year?"
- "How many books have I finished since January?"
- "Which habits have my longest streaks right now?"

**Cross-domain questions:**
- "Do my moods tend to be better after I work out?"
- "What movies did I watch during my last vacation?"
- "Who was I with at my highest-rated restaurant visits?"

**Creating and updating entries:**
- Guide can create events, workouts, tasks, habits, journal entries, and vacations directly from chat
- Describe what you want and Guide handles the data entry

## Models and quotas

Guide supports multiple AI models. A dropdown in the chat header lets you switch between them. Each model has a separate usage quota shown as a progress bar — it turns amber at 75% and red at 90%. If you're running low on one model, switching to another resets your available quota.

## Rich cards

When Guide retrieves structured data — a workout, an event, a task, a vacation — it renders it as a formatted card in the conversation rather than just describing it in text. This makes it easy to review what was found or created.

## Privacy

Everything you discuss with Guide stays within your personal archive. Conversations are stored in your account and are not used to train models.
