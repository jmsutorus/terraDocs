# Achievements

Achievements are milestones you unlock automatically as you use Terra. Log your mood consistently, finish a run streak, visit a national park, read a certain number of books — each milestone earns an achievement badge and a points value called **Curator Points**.

## How it works

Achievements are checked in the background as you use Terra. When you hit a milestone, the achievement unlocks and a notification fires. You don't need to track progress manually — just use the app and achievements accumulate over time.

The Achievements page shows your full catalogue: everything unlocked, everything still locked, your progress toward each one, and your total Curator Points.

## Curator Points

Every achievement has a points value. As you unlock achievements your total Curator Points grows. The points are a running reflection of how broadly and consistently you've used Terra across all its sections.

## Categories

Achievements are organized into 17 categories — one per major section of Terra:

Mood, Habits, Exercise, Tasks, Journals, Media, Parks, Drinks, Restaurants, Events, Vacations, Relationship, People, Goals, Finances, Sports, and General.

Each category has its own set of milestones with progress bars showing how close you are to each one. When you unlock every achievement in a category, a completion banner appears.

## What you can do

- **Browse all achievements** organized by category with quick-jump navigation links
- **See your progress** on locked achievements via individual progress bars
- **Check your Curator Points** total and unlocked count
- **Refresh achievements** with the sync button to check for any recently earned ones that haven't surfaced yet
