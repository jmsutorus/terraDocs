# Settings

Settings is where you configure Terra to fit your preferences — your profile, how the app looks and behaves, notification behaviour, and account security.

## The four sections

| Section | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Profile** | Your personal information — avatar, bio, birthday, body metrics, and location |
| **Appearance** | Theme, haptic feedback, and calendar colour customisation |
| **Notifications** | Push notifications, care alerts, and zen mode |
| **Account & Security** | Integrations, media tag management, storage, and account actions |

Navigate to Settings from the main menu, then tap any card to open that section.

---

## Profile

Update your avatar, bio, birthday, and any body metrics you want to track (height, weight, etc.). Location fields let you set home and work locations for context across Terra.

---

## Appearance

**Theme** — switch between Light and Dark mode.

**Haptic feedback** — toggle device vibration for actions like completing a habit or saving an entry. Useful to disable on devices where vibration is intrusive.

**Calendar colours** — customise which colour represents each data type in the Calendar grid cells. Lets you visually distinguish between moods, workouts, events, and other entry types at a glance.

---

## Notifications

**Push notifications** — enable or disable all push notifications from Terra.

**Care alerts** — custom alert types for specific nudges (e.g., reminders to log your mood, check in on a habit).

**Zen mode** — a do-not-disturb toggle that silences Terra notifications during focused periods.

---

## Account & Security

**Integrations** — connect and manage third-party services linked to Terra.

**Media tags and genres** — manage the custom tags and genre options used across your Media section.

**Storage** — view your current storage usage and quota.

**Install Terra** — a prompt to install Terra as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your device for a native app-like experience.

**Sign out** — signs you out of your current session.

**Delete account** — permanently removes your account and all data. This requires confirmation and cannot be undone.
