# Daily Check-Ins

The daily check-in is the core action in the Habits section — tap to log a completion, and your streak stays alive. This page covers how it works and how to handle edge cases.

## Logging a completion

Your active habits appear on the **home dashboard** in the Daily Rituals card and on the **Habits page**. Each habit has a check-off button next to it.

Tap it when you've done the habit. You'll feel a haptic pulse and see a confirmation. That's it — the completion is logged for today.

**One completion per habit per day.** Tap again to undo a completion if you logged it by mistake.

## What happens when you check in

- The completion is saved to your record
- Your streak updates immediately
- The Pulse progress bar fills to reflect today's completion rate
- If you complete all of your active habits for the day, confetti fires

## Streaks

A streak is the number of consecutive periods you've completed a habit without missing a gap. What counts as a gap depends on your habit's frequency:

| Frequency | Streak breaks when... |
|---|---|
| Daily | You miss a full day |
| Every Other Day | You go more than 2 days without logging |
| Three Times a Week | You don't log 3 times in a calendar week |
| Once a Week | You go 7+ days without logging |
| Monthly | You go 31+ days without logging |

Your **current streak** is shown on each habit card as a flame with the day count. Your **longest streak ever** is visible in the detail panel.

## Logging offline

If you don't have an internet connection, completions queue locally and sync automatically when you're back online. You'll see a notification confirming the sync. Your streak holds — the logged date is what matters, not when it synced.

## Logging a past completion

If you forgot to check in yesterday, you can still log it. Open the habit's detail panel and use the date selector to log for a specific past date. This keeps your history accurate without inflating today's count.

## What shows on the home dashboard

The Daily Rituals card on the home dashboard shows your active habits for today with a completion percentage bar. As you check things off the bar fills. The dashboard is designed for a quick daily scan — tap through your habits, check them off, done.

## Completion rate on the Habits page

At the top of the Habits page, the **Weekly Velocity** percentage shows how many of your possible completions you've logged in the last 7 days. If you have 3 daily habits and completed all of them 5 out of 7 days this week, your velocity is roughly 71%.

Use this as a weekly pulse check — not a grade, just a signal.
