# Explore

Explore is where you discover and configure which sections of Terra are active for you. Every module — Finances, Habits, Parks, Relationship, Media, and the rest — can be equipped to your Basecamp (your main navigation) or unequipped if it's not relevant to your life right now.

## How it works

The Explore page shows every available module in Terra as a card, organised by category. Cards show the module name, a short description, and a preview image. Modules you've already equipped show an **Equipped** badge.

Tap any card to see more detail about the module — what it does, what you can track with it. From the detail view, tap **Equip to Basecamp** to activate it or **Unequip Module** to remove it from your navigation.

## Categories

Modules are grouped into categories:
- Health & Wellness (Mood, Habits, Exercise, Pulse)
- Personal Growth (Goals, Tasks, Journals, Finances)
- Creative Expression (Media, Recipes, Drinks)
- Social (People, Relationship, Events, Restaurants)
- Exploration (Parks, Vacations, Explore)
- And others

## What equipping does

Equipping a module adds it to your main navigation so you can access it quickly from anywhere in Terra. Unequipping hides it from your navigation — your data is never deleted, just hidden from view. You can re-equip at any time.

This lets you keep your Basecamp focused on what matters to you right now. If you're not tracking finances this season, unequip it. If you start a new habit-building phase, equip Habits front and centre.

## Common questions

**If I unequip a module, does my data disappear?**
No. Unequipping only removes the module from your navigation. All your data stays intact. Re-equip the module anytime to access it again.

**Can I equip all modules at once?**
Yes — equip as many as you like. The navigation will show them all.
