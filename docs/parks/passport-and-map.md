# Passport & Map

Two of the Parks tabs turn your visit history into something visual and navigable — the Passport and the Map.

## Passport tab

The Passport tab shows your park visits as collectible stamps, organised by category (National Parks, State Parks, Wilderness, etc.). Each category has a count of how many parks you've logged in it.

This is the most satisfying view for tracking your outdoor progress over time — it makes the collection aspect of park visiting tangible. As you add new parks, new stamps appear.

## Collection tab

The Collection tab shows all your parks as a standard grid — poster image, name, category, state, rating, and visited date. Filter by category or state, or sort by visited date and rating to find specific entries quickly.

## Map tab

The Map tab shows an interactive map of all US National Parks. Parks you've visited are marked distinctly from ones you haven't. This gives you a geographic view of where you've been and where gaps exist.

Tap any park on the map to see its name and your visit status.

## Missing parks

The Parks section tracks which US National Parks you haven't visited yet. Open the **Missing** view to see the full list of unvisited parks. This is useful when planning trips — if you're going to a region, you can see which nearby parks are still missing from your passport.

## Stats on the Home tab

The Home tab shows headline stats for your parks:
- Total park visits logged
- National Parks visited count
- State Parks visited count
- Your three most recent explorations as cards

These update in real time as you add new parks.

## Common questions

**Does the Map show State Parks too?**
The map currently focuses on US National Parks. State parks and other categories appear in the Passport and Collection views but not on the map.

**What counts as a "visit" for the National Parks tracker?**
Any park entry you add with the category set to "National Park" and a visited date counts. The tracker compares your list against the full catalogue of official US National Parks to determine what's missing.

**Can I filter the Passport by state?**
The Passport is organised by category. Use the Collection tab and filter by state to see parks from a specific location.
