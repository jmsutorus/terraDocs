# Parks

Parks is your outdoor adventure archive — a log of every national park, state park, trail, and wilderness area you've visited. Log visits, document the trails you hiked, add photos and companions, and build a passport of your time in nature.

## How it works

Each park gets its own entry with the details of your visit — date, rating, photos, trails hiked, who came with you, and a personal reflection. Terra also tracks which US National Parks you haven't visited yet, so you can plan future adventures from the ones missing from your passport.

## What you can store per park

- **Name** and category (National Park, State Park, Wilderness, National Forest, National Seashore, Monument, Recreation Area, City Park, Other)
- **State** — where it is
- **Visited date**
- **Rating** — 0–10
- **Description** — a short summary
- **Poster image**
- **Tags**
- **Personal quote** — a memorable line from the visit
- **Editorial content** — a full markdown editor for a longer reflection
- **Trails** — individual hikes with distance, elevation gain, difficulty, date, notes, and AllTrails link
- **Photos** — with captions and dates, displayed as a gallery
- **People** — who you visited with

## The five tabs

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Stats overview and recent explorations |
| **Passport** | Your visits as collectible stamps by category |
| **Collection** | Full grid of all your parks |
| **Map** | Interactive map of US National Parks with visited status |
| **Missing** | National Parks you haven't visited yet |

## What you can do

- **Log park visits** with full trail and photo detail
- **Track trails** within each park with difficulty, distance, and elevation
- **Browse your passport** as a visual stamp collection
- **See which National Parks you haven't visited** and plan future trips
- **Tag companions** so visits appear in their People profile
- **Publish parks** to share your outdoor archive publicly
