# The Earthbound Editorial

Welcome to **The Earthbound Editorial**. This is your guide to living with intention, rhythm, and kinetic harmony.

Most people track their life in six different apps. None of them talk to each other. Earthbound is the antidote—a digital sanctuary for the modern archivist where every domain of your life connects into a single, coherent picture.

## The Core Experience

Navigate through the pillars of your Life Archive:

- **[The Pulse](/docs/pulse/overview)**: Your daily dashboard for immediate awareness.
- **[The Ranger](/docs/ai/overview)**: Your sapient AI guide that connects the dots across your history.
- **[The Chronicle](/docs/chronicle/overview)**: A weekly editorial summary of your lived experience.
- **[The Journey](/docs/journey/overview)**: Understanding the mountain-ascent metaphor of your growth.
- **[Explore](/docs/explore/overview)**: Visualizing the patterns and textures of your life.

## Your Life Archive

## Our Philosophy

Every pixel of Earthbound is guided by **The Editorial Standard**. We reject the clinical coldness of modern software in favor of tactile depth, tonal layering, and the complete rejection of harsh, artificial lines. We believe that a well-designed space for self-reflection shapes the quality of the reflection itself.

Your life is not a feed to be consumed. It is a gallery to be curated.

May your journey be grounded.
