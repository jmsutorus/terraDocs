# Grocery List

The Pantry tab is a grocery list that connects directly to your recipes. You can import a full recipe's ingredients with one tap, add items manually, and check things off as you shop.

## Accessing the grocery list

Go to the Recipes section and tap the **Pantry** tab.

## Importing from a recipe

Open the recipe you're planning to cook, then tap **Import to Grocery List**. All of the recipe's ingredients are added to your Pantry immediately, grouped by category (produce, dairy, meat, pantry staples, etc.).

You can import from multiple recipes in one shopping trip — items accumulate in the list and stay grouped by category.

## Adding items manually

Tap the add button in the Pantry tab to enter items that aren't part of a recipe — household supplies, specific brands, or anything else you need.

## Checking off items

Tap any item to mark it as purchased. Checked items are visually distinguished from unchecked ones. As you move through the store, check things off in real time.

## Clearing checked items

Once you're done shopping, use the batch delete option to remove all checked items at once. Unchecked items stay in the list until you clear them manually.

## Tips

- **Import the recipe before you leave for the store**, not after — the ingredient list pulls exactly what the recipe calls for, including quantities and units, so it's ready to reference while you shop.
- **The category grouping matches how most stores are laid out.** Produce together, dairy together, meat together — you can work through the list section by section without backtracking.
- **The grocery list doesn't reset automatically.** Clear it when you're done with a shopping trip so it's empty and ready for the next one.
