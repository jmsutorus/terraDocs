# Relationship

The Relationship section is a private journal for your romantic partnership. It's where you track shared dates, log intimacy, and record the milestones that define your relationship's story. Everything here is always private — none of it is ever published or shared.

## How it works

The Relationship section is built around one person: the person you've marked as your romantic partner in the People section. All entries — dates, intimacy logs, and milestones — are about that relationship.

There are three types of entries:

| Type | What it tracks |
|---|---|
| **Dates** | Shared outings — dinners, movies, activities, concerts, trips |
| **Intimacy** | Private tracking of your intimate life together |
| **Milestones** | Significant moments in your relationship's story |

## The four tabs

- **Dates** — your history of shared experiences, with a featured view of recent ones and a full timeline below
- **Intimacy** — a private log with mood, duration, and other details
- **Milestones** — a timeline of the major chapters in your relationship
- **Manage** — your custom positions library and relationship settings

## Privacy

Every entry in the Relationship section is private by default and cannot be published. The intimacy tab has an additional privacy banner as a reminder. This data is only ever visible to you.

## Getting started

If you haven't already, go to the People section, find your partner's profile (or create one), and toggle **Romantic Partner** on. Once that's set, the Relationship section is tied to them and you can start adding entries.
