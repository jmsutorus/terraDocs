# Media

Media is your personal archive of cultural consumption — movies, TV shows, books, games, and albums you've watched, read, played, or listened to. Rate them, track your progress through ongoing ones, write editorial notes, and build a searchable history of what you've experienced.

## How it works

Each entry has a type, a status, and as much or as little detail as you want to add. Status tracks where you are with something:

- **In Progress** — actively watching, reading, or playing
- **Completed** — finished
- **Planned** — on your list to experience

In Progress entries appear as **Active Journeys** at the top of the Media page — a visual row of cards showing what you're currently working through with a progress percentage.

## What you can store per entry

- **Title** and type (Movie, TV, Book, Game, or Album)
- **Status** — In Progress, Completed, or Planned
- **Rating** — 0–10
- **Started and completed dates**
- **Genres and tags**
- **Poster image**
- **Description**
- **Creator(s)** — author, director, developer, artist
- **Progress percentage** — for in-progress items
- **Achievement percentage** — for games
- **Time spent** — hours logged with this entry
- **Editorial content** — a full markdown editor for your review, reflection, or notes
- **Rankings** — track how this entry fits in your personal ranking system over time

## The four tabs

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Active Journeys and featured entries |
| **Collection** | Full archive with search, filter, and sort |
| **Curation** | Editorial bento-box layouts you build |
| **Agent** | AI recommendations and insights |

## What you can do

- **Log media** as you start or finish it
- **Track progress** through in-progress items
- **Rate and review** with 0–10 ratings and markdown editorial notes
- **Filter by type** — see only books, only films, etc.
- **Sort by** rating, title, date completed, or date started
- **Create custom rankings** to track your personal tier lists over time
- **Publish entries** to share your media archive on your public profile

*Curate what you consume.*
