# Tracking Progress

The Goals page gives you a snapshot of your active pursuits. The Progress page goes deeper — stats, category breakdowns, and a milestone view organized by urgency.

## The Goals page

Your active goals appear in two sections:

- **Spotlight** — the goal furthest along gets an expanded card at the top with its full description and a progress bar. This changes as your goals advance.
- **Active Aspirations** — the rest of your active goals in a grid. Each card shows the title, description preview, priority, tags, milestone count, and progress ring.

Below those, **Recently Manifested** lists your completed goals in order of most recently updated.

## Progress rings

Every goal card shows a circular progress ring that fills as you complete checklist items and milestones. The color indicates how far along you are:

- **Green** — 75%+ complete or fully completed
- **Amber** — 50–74% complete
- **Default** — under 50%

## The Progress page

Navigate to the Progress page for a fuller picture across all your goals.

### Stats tiles
Four numbers at a glance:
- **Active goals** — count of Not Started + In Progress + On Hold
- **Completed goals** — total goals marked Completed
- **Average progress** — mean completion % across your active goals
- **Overdue** — goals with a passed target date not yet marked complete

### Status distribution
A horizontal bar showing the proportion of all your goals by status — how much is active vs. completed vs. on hold. Useful for seeing whether your goals list is balanced or whether things are piling up in one state.

### By category
A chart of your top goal tags showing which areas you have the most goals in. If Health and Career dominate while Learning has one entry, that's visible here.

### Milestones by urgency
The Progress page organizes all your outstanding milestones into buckets based on their target dates:

| Bucket | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Overdue** | Target date passed, not yet complete |
| **This Week** | Due within 7 days |
| **This Month** | Due within 30 days |
| **Later** | Due further out |
| **No Date** | No target date set |
| **Completed** | Finished milestones, shown for reference |

Use this view to make sure you're not ignoring a milestone that's slipping past its date.

## Filtering on the Goals page

Use the filter controls to narrow your view:

- **Status** — show only goals in a specific status (all statuses, or pick one)
- **Priority** — show only High, Medium, or Low priority goals

By default, Archived and Abandoned goals are hidden. Select those statuses explicitly if you want to see them.

## Common questions

**My progress ring isn't moving. Why?**
Progress is based on completed checklist items and milestones. If your goal has no checklists or milestones, there's nothing to calculate. Add at least a basic checklist to start tracking progress.

**A goal is 100% but I haven't marked it Completed. Does that matter?**
Progress percentage and status are separate. You can have a fully-checked checklist without changing the status. When you're satisfied the goal is genuinely done, change the status to Completed — it moves the goal to the Recently Manifested section and captures a completion date.

**How do I find goals I abandoned months ago?**
Use the Status filter on the Goals page and select Abandoned. Your abandoned goals will appear and you can reactivate or delete them.
